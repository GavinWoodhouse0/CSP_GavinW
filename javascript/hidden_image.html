Facts About Hazelnuts!

Did you know that Hazelnuts are, in fact, NOT Macademia nuts? Actually, here's a list of things that Hazelnuts are not.

    Hazelnuts are NOT:
  • Macademia nuts.
  • Chestnuts.
  • Made of chocolate.

Hazelnuts, also, can be made into a chocolate spread, but this does not mean that this spread is automatically good for you!
